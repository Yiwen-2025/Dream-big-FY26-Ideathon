🧠
Cognitive Drain

The Vision

Imagine a New Way of Working

When you open your computer,
you don't open apps.
❌ Old Paradigm
App-Centric Work
  • Open email, then CRM, then docs
  • Switch context constantly
  • Manually track what to do next
  • Cognitive overload every morning
→
✅ New Paradigm
Human-Centric Intelligence
  • AI greets you with today's plan

The Solution

What Work Sphere Does

📋
Daily & Weekly Plan Presents today's and this week's prioritized work automatically at start-of-day.
📁
Case Awareness Surfaces assigned JE cases, CRM cases, and tickets in one unified view.
⏰
Pending Actions Highlights approvals, blockers, dependencies, and overdue items proactively.
🔐
Background Integration Automatically accesses authorized tools — no manual switching required.
Contextual Prompts Gentle, intelligent guidance on what to do next, right when you need it.
Easy Reprioritization Supports simple daily and weekly reviews with drag-and-drop reprioritization.
